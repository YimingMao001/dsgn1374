Interview

Q: Why you want to be a graphic designer?

Yiming: It’s fun, you can create a lot of things and apply a lot of ideas; every time I saw my works, I feel a sense of accomplishment.

Q: What you think that a designer should do?

Yiming: Fulfill customer’s requirements and communicate with people by our design.

Q: Are there any difficulties that you ae facing in your study process?

Yiming: I feel like that I need to improve my drawing skills.

Q: What is you understand of web?

Yiming: It’s a way that people convey information to others, and because it is online’ it won’t limited by distance.

Q: Do you want to be a web designer in the future?

Yiming: I’m not sure. It feels like a challenging job, but it is pretty fun thought, I’m willing to give a shot.
